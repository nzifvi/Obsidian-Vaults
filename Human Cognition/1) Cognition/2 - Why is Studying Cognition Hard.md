# 1) Cognition cannot be directly observed
Cognitive processes cannot directly be observed, it is like a black box.
- Inputs go in and outputs come out.
- No idea what happens in between.

The cognitive processes have to be inferred via relationships between the inputs and outputs

# 2) Studies involving Cognition can be unethical/impractical
Studies, such as lesion studies (where some neurons are removed from a neural network), would be unethical and or impractical to perform on humans
