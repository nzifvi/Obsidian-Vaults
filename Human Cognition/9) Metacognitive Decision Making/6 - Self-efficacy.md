# Self-efficacy
Self-efficacy is the belief in one's ability to execute a specific goal-orientated behaviour.
- E.g. believing you have X level of ability at Y task.
- Self-efficacy is a subjective evaluation of competence in a type of task.

Self-efficacy is a determining factor of motivation and effort.
- As level of self-efficacy increases, more likely to work hard on a task.
- As level of self-efficacy decreases, less likely to work hard on a task.

# Measuring Self-efficacy
Self-efficacy is measured by assessing which task people think they will perform better in.
- The better somebody believes they will perform, the higher their self-efficacy.
