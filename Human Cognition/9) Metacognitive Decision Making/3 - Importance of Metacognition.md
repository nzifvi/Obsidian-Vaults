# Importance of Evaluation in Metacognition
## In Humans:
Metacognitive evaluation supports.
1) Group decisions and knowledge sharing.
2) Knowing when to act or commit to a decision.
3) Deciding whether it is possible to complete a task in a reasonable timeframe.
4) Reflecting on thoughts so that you can explain thought/decision processes.


## In AI:
Metacognitive evaluation supports:
1) Communicating confidence to users of decision support systems.
2) Determining whether it is possible to complete a task in a reasonable timeframe.
3) Making AI able to explain itself.


# Importance of Control in Metacognition
## In Humans:
Metacognitive control supports:
1) Selecting and executing strategies.


## In AI:
Metacognitive control supports:
1) Selecting solvers or strategies to a given problem.
