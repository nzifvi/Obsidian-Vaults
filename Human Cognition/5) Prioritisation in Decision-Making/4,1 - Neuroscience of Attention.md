# Dorsal Attention Network (DAN)
DAN is a distributed set of brain regions with correlated activity.
- DAN aka task-positive network (TPN)

![[Pasted image 20260311104950.png]]

DAN is associated with visuo-spatial attention and orienteering of attention (e.g. allocating gaze)

Regions of DAN (FEF, SC, LIP), are known to be involved with attention because inactivating neurons in these regions reduces an animal's ability to successfully perform attentional tasks.

# Default-Mode Network (DMN)
DMN is a distributed network of brain regions associated with...
- Mind-wandering.
- Daydreaming.
- Rest.
- Inattention.
- Self-reflection
