# What is Endogenous Attention?
Endogenous (from inside) is self-directed focus.
- A type of attention under executive control: attention is allocated to something.

Endogenous attention aka...
1) Volitional attention.
2) Top-down attention.

Endogenous attention is driven by...
1) Task demands.
2) Goals.
3) Rewards.
4) Behavioural relevance.
