# Hemispatial Neglect
Hemispatial neglect can occur following a stroke to the posterior parietal regions.
- Affected hemifield is "neglected"
- Neglected means that visuospatial attention is not paid there.

![[Pasted image 20260311115955.png]]

# Ways to test for Hemispatial Neglect
## 1) Cancellation Test
Works by searching for specific targets. None are found on the contralateral side DESPITE actually being there.
- Contralateral side = neglected side.
![[Pasted image 20260311120301.png]]


## 2) Patients ignore features on the contralateral side of their vision
- Contralateral side is the neglected side.
