# What is Divided Attention?
Divided attention is the splitting up of attention and paying attention to multiple things simultaneously.
- Aka dual-task attention.
- Aka multi-tasking attention.

To measure divided attention, instruct somebody to do 2 or more things at once.
- If tasks interfere with each other, performance/response speeds will decrease as attention is divided.

Measuring divided attention demonstrates that attention is not an infinite resource.
- Performance/response speeds decrease as attention resource becomes used up.
- However, if you are motivated, divided attention increases as you can perform/respond better
