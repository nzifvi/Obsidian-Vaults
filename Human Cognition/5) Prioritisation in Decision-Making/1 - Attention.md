# What is Attention?
Attention is a characteristic and property of multiple perceptual and cognitive mechanisms. 

The core characteristic of attention are...
1) It has limited capacity.
	- Cannot pay attention to everything simultaneously.
	- There is not a hard limit of how much attention can be paid. It depends on context.
2) It is a selection or priority mechanism.
	- Function of attention is to select information that will be consequently prioritised.
	- Information might be regions of space, external stimuli/stimulus features, internal states (thoughts), etc.
3) It is modulatory.
	- Process of paying attention does something to object of attention: has consequences.
	- Consequences are perceptual: attention changes stimulus processing.
4) It is subject to vigilance.
	- Attention can be sustained over time.

# Variants of Attention
1) Feature-based attention & visual search.
2) Exogeneous & Endogenous Attention
3) Spatial attention
	- Feature-based attention.
4) Multi-tasking
	- Dividing attention to multiple things.
