# Perception as Inference
Inputs to the visual system are 2D but visual perception is 3D.
- Some element of construction or inference exists in perception.
- Perception is thought of as indirect

# Perception as Inference: Depth Perception
## Binocular Cues
One eye handles...
1) Retinal disparity (difference in images)
2) Convergence (difference in retinal angle).

## Monocular Cues
Two eyes handle...
1) Accommodation (change of focus)
2) Relative size.
3) Motion parallax (apparent motion)
