# 2) Constraints on our Decision-Making

1) We have limited cognitive resources.
	- Cognitive resources, like attention and memory, are finite.
2) We have limited time.
	- Decisions must often be made within an amount of time.
3) We have limited attempts.
	- Once a decision is made, it cannot often be remade.
	- Cannot test a large number of approaches. Once an approach is used it normally has to be stuck with.
4) We have limited information.
	- Knowledge about a situation is often incomplete.
	- Decisions must be made without full information.
