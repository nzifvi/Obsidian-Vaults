# DDM vs SDT
## Similarities

1) Both DDM and SDTs are used to model 2 choice decisions.

2) Both have thresholds where a choice is made, based on evidence.

3) BOTH can be used to measure sensitivity (drift and dprime) and bias (starting point & decision threshold).

4) BOTH are silent on mechanism

## Differences
1) DDM models evidence over time. SDT is static.

2) SDT has only 2 parameters. DDM has more.
	- CANNOT estimate all of DDM's parameters from one data set.
