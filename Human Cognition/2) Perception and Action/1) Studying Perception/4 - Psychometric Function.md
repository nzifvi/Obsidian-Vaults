# What is the Psychometric Function
Psychometric functions are a type of function which represents the data from psychophysical tasks.
- 2D function: y-axis is proportion of correct responses, x axis are values of the visual stimulus being controlled over the course of the research

$$
T_x
$$
Let $T_x$ denote the responses from participants whilst the visual stimuli's parameter is at value x.
$$
(answer_{given}, answer_{true}) \in T_x
$$
- $answer_{given}$ is the answer given by a research participant.
- $answer_{true}$ is the actual answer.

Let $correct(T_x)$ be equal to the number of given answers which are equal to the true answers.
$$
p(x) = \frac{correct(T_x)}{|T_x|}
$$
