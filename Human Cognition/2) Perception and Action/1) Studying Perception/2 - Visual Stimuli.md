# Visual Stimuli
Visual stimuli used for psychophysical experiments.
- Some property of the visual stimuli is manipulated as the control variable.

Idea of visual stimuli: show most simple thing possible.
- More complex a stimuli, harder it is to figure out why they give an answer.
# Types of Visual Stimuli

#### 1) Gaussian Blob
Gaussian blobs are good for testing detection.
- Can blob be seen.
- Where is the blob.

![[Pasted image 20260207152343.png]]

Gaussian blobs have no edges.
- Reduces assistance in detection.

You can manipulate...
- Duration a blob appears for.
- Intensity a blob appears with.
- Location a blob appears at
... as control variables.
#### 2) Gabor Patch
Gabor patches are good for testing V1 simple cells.
- V1 simple are edge detector cells.
- Gabor patches are a good for testing edge detection.

![[Pasted image 20260207152637.png]]

You can manipulate...
- Orientation (tilt).
- Contrast.
- Spatial frequency (how many appear within a space).
- Location
#### Random Dot Kinematogram (RDK)
RDKs are useful for studying perception of motion

![[Pasted image 20260207153024.png]]

You can manipulate:
- Direction of motion of dots.
- Direction of their motion.
- Coherence (the % of dots moving in non-random directions)
