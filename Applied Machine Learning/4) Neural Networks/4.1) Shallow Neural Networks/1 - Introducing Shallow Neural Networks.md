# What is a Shallow Neural Network?
Shallow neural networks are functions f with parameters $\vec{\phi}$ which maps multivariable inputs, $\vec{x}$, to outputs $\vec{y}$
$$
\vec{y}=f(\vec{\phi}, \vec{x})
$$
$$
\vec{\phi}_{n\times1}= \begin{bmatrix}
\phi_0\\
...\\
\phi_n
\end{bmatrix}, \vec{x}_{(n-1)\times1} = \begin{bmatrix}
x_1\\
...\\
x_{n}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
