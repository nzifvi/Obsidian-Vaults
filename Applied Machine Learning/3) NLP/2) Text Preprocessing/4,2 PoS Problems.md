# 1) Shallow form of Processing
PoS tagging is a shallow form of processing.
- Only 1 tag per word when a word might belong to multiple grammatical classes.
- No larger structures are created.

# 2) PoS Ambiguity
A word could potentially be assigned multiple PoS tags.
- This makes PoS ambiguous. 

2 types of ambiguity exists:
1) Local Ambiguity.
2) Global Ambiguity.

## Local Ambiguity
Local ambiguity is mean that words on their own may have multiple possible PoS tags assigned to them.

Most words are locally ambiguous.
- Intended PoS can be determined from context given in the entire sentence.
