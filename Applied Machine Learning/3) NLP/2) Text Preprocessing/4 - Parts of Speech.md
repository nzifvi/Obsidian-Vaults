# What are Parts of Speech (POS)
Words can be categorised based on how they behave grammatically.
- Linguists define 9-10 lexical classes

Grammatical classes are defined as:
1) Adjective
2) Adverb
3) Auxiliary Verb
4) Conjunction
5) Determiner
6) Interjection
7) Noun
8) Preposition
9) Pronoun
10) Verb

Each word, in a given sentence, will belong to one of these grammatical classes.


POS is useful as...
1) Can help disambiguate words for below tasks.
	- Information retrieval.
	- Text-to-speech
	- Document classification.

2) Tells us what words are likely to occur nearby.
	- Adjectives often followed by a given noun for example.
	- Personal pronouns often followed by a given verb for example.
3) It is important to identify larger grammatical structure.
