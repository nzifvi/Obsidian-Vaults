# Distributional Hypothesis
Words which occur in the same contexts will normally have similar, if not identical, meanings.

## Example
> "A bottle of Tezguino is on the table"
> "Everybody likes Tezguino"
> "Tezguino makes you drunk"
> "Tezguino is made out of corn"

The contexts the word Tezguino is used in suggests that:
- Tezguino is a type of alcoholic drink.
- Tezguino is made out corn.
