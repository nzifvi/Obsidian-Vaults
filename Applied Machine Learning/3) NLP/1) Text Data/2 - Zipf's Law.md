# 1) What is Zipf's Law?
The product of a word's frequency and it's rank (ordering by frequency) is approximately constant.
$$
freq(word) \propto \frac{1}{rank(word)}
$$
While only a few words are used very frequently.
- Many, or most, words are used very infrequently.

Idea of Zipf's Law is that if the most frequent word, such that $rank(word)=1$, has a $freq(word) = 100$, then the 2nd most frequent word will occur 50 times.
- etc etc.
