# 1) What is Herdan-Heaps' Law?
Herdan-Heaps' Law is the idea that, the larger the corpora, the more word types will be found.
$$
|V| = kN^{\beta}
$$
$$
\forall k, k \in \mathbb{R}|k>0
$$
$$
\forall \beta, \beta \in \mathbb{R}|0<\beta<1
$$
Values of $k$ and $\beta$ depend on the domain and genre.
- $k=1, \beta = 0.7$ are typically sufficient values for general purpose corpora.
- for restricted domains, values should be lower.
