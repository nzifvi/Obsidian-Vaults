## What is Knowledge
Knowledge is the understanding of rules in order to interpret, and use, information.
- Obtained through education and experience.

### Example
Information: $f(x) = 4x^{2}+8x+4 = 0$

Knowledge would be the understanding that the information is a quadratic function and then the understanding of the rules that can be applied to the quadratic function to extract additional information from it.


## What are Knowledge Workers?
Knowledge workers are those with specialist knowledge that makes them "experts"
- A person becomes a knowledge worker through formal and informal rules they learned via education and or experience.

Examples of knowledge workers are scientists, doctors, managers, librarians, etc.
