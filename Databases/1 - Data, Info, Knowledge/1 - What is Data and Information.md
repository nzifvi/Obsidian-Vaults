## What is Data?
Data is a collection, or a single piece, of unprocessed facts or figures.
- Numbers, strings, characters, boolean, values. Anything.

Data by itself has no meaning.

## Data -> Information
To make data useful, it must be processed into meaningful information and then presented in the most useful format.
- Data must be processed in a CONTEXT in order to give it meaning.

![[Pasted image 20250524124555.png]]

Suppose you are given a piece of data: 12.
- You do not know what it is, or what meaning it has, until you are given context (more information) about the data.

Suppose you are told to process the data, 12, as an hour.
- You can now say 12 is an hour.

Then, present the information in the most useful format: 12:00.


## What is Information?
Information is data that has been processed, within a context frame, in order to give it meaning.
- 12 pounds is the average hourly income of the sample set.

OR

Data that has been processed into a form which gives it meaning.
- Time (12:00)
