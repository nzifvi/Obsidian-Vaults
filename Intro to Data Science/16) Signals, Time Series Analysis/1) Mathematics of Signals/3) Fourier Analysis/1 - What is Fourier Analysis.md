# What is Fourier Analysis?
Fourier analysis is the 

The idea of Fourier analysis is that any signal can be represented as a sum of sine and cosine waves of different...
- Frequencies
- Amplitudes
- Phases

$$
x(t) \approx \sum_{i=0}^{n}\biggr[A_icos(2\pi\cdot f_{i}\cdot \phi_{i})\biggr]
$$
Where...
- $A_{i}$ is the amplitude of the $i^{th}$ term.
- $f_{i}$ is the fundamental frequency of the $i^{th}$ term.
- $\phi_{i}$ is the phase shift of the $i^{th}$ term.
