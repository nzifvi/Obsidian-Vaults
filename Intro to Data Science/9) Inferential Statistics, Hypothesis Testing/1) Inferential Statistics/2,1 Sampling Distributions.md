# What are Sampling Distributions?
Sampling distributions is a distribution of a descriptive statistic from many random samples created from the sample population.
- Samples must be equally sized.

Let $\ohm$ denote the population
$$
X_1 \in \ohm \And X_2 \in \ohm \And X_3 \in \ohm | |X_1| = |X_2| = |X_3|
$$
$$
X_1 = \{12, 14, 7, 9, 10\}
$$
$$
X_2 = \{9, 12, 7, 4, 5\}
$$
$$
X_3 = \{12, 12, 15, 19, 5\}
$$
A Sampling distribution of the sample's means, S, can be denoted as:

$$
S=\{\bar{X_1}, \bar{X_2}, \bar{X_3}\}
$$
