# Types of Clustering Algorithms
Two types of clustering algorithms exist:
1) Partition Algorithms.
2) Hierarchical Algorithms.

## Partition Algorithms.
No hierarchy is enforced. Sample is clustered and no substructures are attempted to be found.

## Hierarchical Algorithms.
Hierarchical algorithms attempt to find subclusters of clusters whilst clustering.
