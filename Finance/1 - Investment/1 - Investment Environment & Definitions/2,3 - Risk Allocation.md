## Risk Allocation
All real assets involve an amount of risk.
- When Audi builds auto plants, it cannot know 100% which cash flows the auto plants will generate.

Financial markets, and the securities traded within those markets, allow investors to make use of different securities based on the amount of risk they are willing to subject themselves.
- More optimistic and risk tolerant investors can buy shares of stock of abc corporation, knowing the potential that it can all go wrong.
- Less optimistic and risk tolerant investors can instead buy bonds which promise to provide a fixed-income payment.
