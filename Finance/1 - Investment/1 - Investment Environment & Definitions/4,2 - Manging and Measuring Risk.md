## Manging and Measuring Risk
When managing a portfolio, the risk of it should be managed and measured.
- It is difficult and complex to measure risk, how volatile an investment is will only give you part of a measure of risk.
- Risk measurement is multi-variable.

When asset classes are mixed into a diversified portfolio, you must consider...
- How the asset classes being mixed relate and interact with each other.
- How the individual securities relate and interact with each other.
- How diversification effects portfolio risk.
- The implications of proper risk measurement.
