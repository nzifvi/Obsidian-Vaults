# Notes

A study which investigated the application of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), a type of neural network, found that CNNs result in significant inference costs being incurred during a robot's runtime.
- Requires more powerful, energy consuming GPUs to run models: forcing considerations to be made just to facilitate the CNN.

CNNs in tandem with visual SLAM have high compatibility. The use of a CNN enables three-dimensional predictions which further enhance the ability for an environment to be mapped.
