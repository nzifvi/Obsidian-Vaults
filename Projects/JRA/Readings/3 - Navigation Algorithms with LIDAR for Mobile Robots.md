# Limitations of LiDAR with Traditional Methods

## Data Processing Speed
Smaller microcontrollers do not possess the facilities to handle, and store, the large amount of data produced by LiDAR sensors whilst also coordinating motion and path planning.
- An approach path planning, which encodes environment into the solution, is a desired approach.
