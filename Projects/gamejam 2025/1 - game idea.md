# game idea

- Combat 2d platformer where managing motion is main feature.
	- Motion builds up kinetic energy. Kinetic energy is then used to fuel your attacks.
	- Hitting walls results in a decrease of kinetic energy.
		- Bounce off surface that you came into contact with based on angle of approach and velocity vector.
	- Using attacks results in decrease of kinetic energy.
		- Thresholds of KE unlocks different types of attacks.
