## What is Socrates?

Socrates is a patient treatment simulator, designed to be realistic.

Simulator contains a wide range of conditions that a patient can attend an ER, or be in another department, for.

Simulator will have different roles, selectable from the main menu, as well as departments that role can be in to further limit what conditions can appear.
- Example: nurse working in ER, doctor working in ICU. etc.

The player, upon entering a mission, will.
- Be given initial diagnostic information from a nurse, HCA, or something else (could be patient themselves).
- Have to treat the patient based on that initial diagnostic information, either helping or killing them.
- Player can gather more diagnostic information, using tests/scans/questions, whilst the patient remains conscious.
