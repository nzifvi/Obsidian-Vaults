# What is Stochastic Optimisation?

Stochastic Optimisation, SO, methods are a set of optimisation methods that generate and use random variables.

SO methods optimise differently to gradient descent based optimisation methods.
- SO does not require a derivative of the fitness function. Gradient descent does.
- Sometimes, you cannot provide derivatives of a fitness function.

SO methods are more flexible. However, GD is more powerful.
