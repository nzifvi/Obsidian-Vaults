# Discrete Alleles
A discrete allele is a set of values which a gene, in a genotype, can take on.

$$

A=\{a_1,...,a_{|A|}\}
$$
For some gene, $g$, part of a genotype.

$$
\forall a, P(g=a)=\frac{1}{|A|}
$$
