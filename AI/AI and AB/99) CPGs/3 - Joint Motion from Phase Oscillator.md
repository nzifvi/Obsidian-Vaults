# Joint Motion
$$
\phi(t)=\omega t + \phi_0
$$

To get joint motion, $\theta (t)$, at time t, map phase to a sine wave:

$$
\theta(t) = Asin\biggr(\phi(t)+\mathcal{Y}\biggr)
$$
Variables of joint motion equation
- $\mathcal{Y}$ - resting joint angle.
