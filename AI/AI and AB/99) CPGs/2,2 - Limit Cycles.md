# What are Limit Cycles?
A limit cycle is an __isolated, closed trajectory__

Isolated means that neighbouring trajectories are not closed. They either...
- Spiral toward limit cycle.
- Spiral away from limit cycle.

![[Pasted image 20260224143144.png]]

A limit cycle can either be STABLE, UNSTABLE, or HALF-STABLE.
- If all neighbouring trajectories approach limit cycle, limit cycle is stable aka attracting.
- Else the limit cycle is unstable.

Stable limit cycles continue to oscillate without inputs forcing their oscillation periodically.


Limit cycles are nonlinear: cannot occur in linear systems.
