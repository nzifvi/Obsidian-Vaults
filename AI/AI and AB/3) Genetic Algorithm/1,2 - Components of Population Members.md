# Phenotype
Phenotype is what undergoes natural selection.
- The genotype codes for the phenotype.

Phenotype can be influenced by other factors such as environmental influences.
- Not just influenced by genotype.

The genotype codes for typically.
- Body
- Brain in artificial evolution.

In genetic algorithms, the phenotype is WHAT the genotype encodes for.
- If genotype encodes for parameters of a car, phenotype is the car.
