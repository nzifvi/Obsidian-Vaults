## Useful Strategies to avoid Mistakes in Probability
Below are some useful strategies to consider, and apply, when handling probability.

#### 1) Simulation
Instead of debating, without anything concrete in the end, it is more useful to study problems by simulations.

Can simulate using a programming language.

#### 2) Biohazards, aka Hazards
Studying common mistakes allows for an understanding of what is and is not valid reasoning in probability to be gained.

#### 3) Sanity Checks
Whilst solving a problem, always make sure what you are doing makes sense to do.

After solving a problem, try to solve the same problem in a different way OR check if the answer, from the problem, makes sense in both simple and extreme cases.
